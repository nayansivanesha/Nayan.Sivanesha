About Me

Student | Social Activist | Rural Development Champion

I am Nayan Talekar, a Class XI student with a passion for social service and rural development. From a young age, I have been deeply involved in community initiatives, learning how focused action can bring meaningful change. Through my work with Model Action For Rural Change, I have participated in projects related to water management, education, and agricultural improvement.


Beyond social work, I am constantly seeking knowledge in areas like psychology, leadership, and sustainable development, aiming to blend innovative ideas with practical solutions. My goal is to create a positive impact that lasts beyond individual projects, shaping the lives of many in my community.

My Work

My work primarily revolves around creating practical solutions for real-life community challenges. I have actively contributed to initiatives like water conservation projects, rural infrastructure development, and educational awareness campaigns.

Atal Bhujal Yojana

Contributing to the successful implementation of the Atal Bhujal Yojana in Solapur district, which has helped improve groundwater management for farmers.

Model Villages

Engaging in creating model villages, integrating technology and government schemes to make rural life more sustainable and efficient.

Community Projects

Every project I take on is guided by a simple principle: impact matters more than recognition. By combining strategy, hands-on effort, and community participation, I aim to inspire others to take part in social change.
